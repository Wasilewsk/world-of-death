# WORLD OF DEATH - TUTORIAL

Welcome to the future of global conflict. You lead 'The Global Vanguard' in a world of emerging nations, robotic armies, and digital warfare.

## CORE CONTROLS (World Map)
- UP/DOWN ARROWS: Navigate menus.
- LEFT/RIGHT ARROWS: Travel between different nations.
- ENTER: Select options.
- ESCAPE: Open Pause Menu (Save Game) or go back.

## ACTION KEYS
- C: COMBAT - Launch a military campaign against the current nation. (Costs 50 Resources).
- D: DIPLOMACY - Offer trades or propose alliances to increase trust.
- S: STEAL - Attempt to sabotage the nation for Resources and Wood. (Success depends on Spy Network).
- H: HACK - Open the Cyber Warfare console for DDoS attacks or Data Breaches.
- G: GATHER - Enter the Forest to manually find Wood and Metal.
- F: CRAFT/R&D - Build robots, recruit soldiers, and research advanced technology.

## RESOURCE GATHERING (The Forest)
- W, A, S, D: Move through the grid.
- RADAR PULSE: Beeps faster and higher as you get closer to WOOD.
- PANNING: If the beep is in your left ear, the wood is to your left!
- METAL CHIME: Listen for a high-pitched chime to find Metal deposits.
- AXIS DRONE: A very high-pitched pulse plays when you are perfectly lined up with the wood (same row or column).
- SPACE: Verbal direction and coordinates.

## BUILDING YOUR EMPIRE
- RECRUITS: Permanently increase military power (+10 each).
- ROBOTS:
    * Gathering Robot: Auto-collects wood/metal every turn.
    * Stealing Robot: Auto-siphons resources from other nations.
    * War Robot: Auto-defends and increases power every turn.
- CYBER HUBS: Increases your Hacking Skill passively.
- ECONOMIC HUBS: Provides passive resource income.

## SURVIVAL TIPS
- GLOBAL TENSION: The longer you play, the more dangerous the world becomes.
- AI HACKING: Watch out for Cyber Alerts (Alarm sound)! Enemies will hack you back if they don't trust you.
- NEW NATIONS: Stay alert for news of new powers emerging like Mars Colony Alpha.

Good luck, Commander.
